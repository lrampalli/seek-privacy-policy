SEEK - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Introduction

SEEK ("we", "our", "us") is a mobile application that connects like-minded college students through peer-nomination questions. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information: Phone number (for authentication via OTP), name, gender, college, course, year, and section.

Contacts: With your permission, we access your phone contacts solely to help you find and invite classmates to SEEK. Contact data is used only for matching and is not stored on our servers beyond invite records.

Usage Data: Questions answered, nominations made, and app interactions to provide the core SEEK experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account via phone OTP
  • To match you with classmates at your college
  • To deliver peer-nomination questions and results
  • To send push notifications about nominations you receive
  • To allow you to invite classmates to join SEEK

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (authentication) and Supabase (database) with row-level security policies. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen in the app. This permanently removes all your data from our systems.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (phone verification)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (push notifications)
  • Supabase (database)
  • UXCam (analytics)
