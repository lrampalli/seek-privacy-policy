SEEK - Delete Your Account

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your SEEK account directly from the app by following these steps:

  1. Open the SEEK app
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your profile information (name, phone number, college details)
  • Your nominations and question responses
  • Your invite records
  • Your notification history
  • Your Firebase authentication account

Data Retention

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your personal data is removed immediately from our active systems. Residual copies in encrypted backups may persist for up to 24 months before being automatically purged.
